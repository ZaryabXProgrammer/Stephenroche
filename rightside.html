THE BOOK

Uncovering the harrowing stories of Shine’s real heroes – our clients, from co-founder Stephen Roche.

What's Inside?

Listening to the voices of those seeking justice has always been at the heart of Shine Lawyers.

Now, Shine is picking up the talking stick to tell its story from modest beginnings as a general law practice courting disaster with every battle to becoming a formidable voice for those who have been harmed or wronged.

This book provides an insider’s view into the real heroes of this journey – Shine’s clients, and the battles and obstacles they have faced in taking legal action, often as a last resort, against injustice inflicted by the powerful and the connected. Some have won, some have not. All have left an imprint on Shine.
